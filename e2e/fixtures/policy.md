# Failed Payment Refund Policy

When a restaurant's weekly payout fails to execute, Support must reissue the payment
within 30 days of the failed payment date. A payment that was charged to the customer
but never settled to the restaurant is always treated as a failed payout: open a billing
ticket, retry the gateway, and reissue the payout manually if the retry does not clear.

Never auto-release money. Finance is propose-only — a human approves every reissue.
